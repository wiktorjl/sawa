**🔴 Weak Performers:**

2. **Healthcare Services** - Hospital & medical service plans struggling (-8%)
3. **Consumer Discretionary** - Entertainment sector (Netflix) down significantly (-12%)
4. **Financials** - Investment advice and life insurance sectors lagging (-6% and -4%)

### Notable Observations

1. **SanDisk (SNDK) Standout Performance**
   - Exceptional +123% YTD return
   - Far outpacing the second-place winner by 5x
   - Driving the Computers & Peripherals subsector to +26% average

2. **Technology Bifurcation**
   - Hardware (semiconductors, equipment) performing well
   - Software and services significantly underperforming
   - Suggests market preference for tangible assets over SaaS models

3. **Energy Resurgence**
   - All energy subsectors posting positive returns
   - Oil majors (XOM, CVX) showing strong performance
   - Reflects potential commodity price strength or supply dynamics

4. **Large Cap Software Selloff**
   - Major enterprise software names (APP, INTU, NOW, ORCL, CRM) all down 17-36%
   - Combined market cap of ~$1T experiencing significant correction
   - May indicate valuation reset or growth concerns

5. **Defensive Strength**
   - Consumer staples (food, tobacco, household products) resilient
   - Utilities showing mixed performance
   - Suggests some risk-off sentiment in the market
